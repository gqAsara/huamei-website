## Outline

### Intro (60–90 words)

- One-sentence definitive answer: Working with a Chinese packaging manufacturer used to mean a 5,000-piece minimum, a twelve-week wait, and a pallet of inventory in a warehouse you do not own — terms that no longer match how luxury brands and small US founders ship.
- One sentence anchoring to Huamei: Across four factories — Henan, Zhejiang, Sichuan, Guizhou — Huamei has shipped luxury rigid boxes to US clients like Serenjoy Box LLC for years; most US-bound work runs out of the Henan press floor.

### H2 — What "working with a Chinese manufacturer" actually means today (120–180 words)

- **Definitive first sentence:** The 2010s playbook — five-thousand-piece minimums, twelve-week waits, anonymous wholesalers — is no longer how a luxury Chinese packaging factory works with a US brand.
- Specifics: MOQs starting at 200 pieces for rigid construction (vs. 5,000+ for offset folding); English-speaking project managers; ISO 9001:2015 quality systems; sample-first iteration; daily progress photos via email and WeChat; FOB Shanghai or Ningbo with US-side import-of-record handled by the brand's customs broker.
- Tie to Huamei: We hold ninety-nine structures, seventeen foils, and eighty papers on file across the four press floors. The conversation a US founder has with us is mostly the same conversation they would have with a US printer — with a different time zone and a longer freight leg.

### H2 — How does working with a Chinese packaging manufacturer work? (featured-snippet target)

- **30–40 word answer block (place immediately under H2):**
  > Working with a Chinese packaging manufacturer runs in five phases — brief, sample, production, pre-shipment QC, and ocean freight. From locked artwork to a US warehouse, expect 7–10 weeks for luxury rigid construction. MOQs start at 200 pieces.
- Then 2–3 supporting paragraphs walking the five phases:
  1. **Brief and quote** (1–2 business days). Artwork files, structure choice, finish list, MOQ, target ship date.
  2. **Sample** (7–10 days). One physical sample on the chosen stock with the chosen foil and closure. Approve or iterate.
  3. **Production** (15–20 days). Press run, decoration, hand-assembly, inspection.
  4. **Pre-shipment QC** (1–2 days). Random sample inspection at our floor; client-side QC firm optional.
  5. **Ocean freight** (~3–4 weeks Shanghai/Ningbo to US West Coast; ~5–6 weeks to East Coast).

### H2 — Quality, certifications, and references (120–180 words)

- **Definitive first sentence:** A serious Chinese luxury packaging manufacturer answers three questions on the first call — where the certifications are scanned, what equipment the press floor runs, and which named clients are willing to be referenced.
- Specifics: ISO 9001:2015 quality management ([standard reference](https://www.iso.org/iso-9001-quality-management.html)), Heidelberg and KBA offset presses on the floor, magnetic pull-force calibrated to a 6–50 g range at 2,800 Gauss, registration on hot-foil-and-emboss held to ±0.1 mm.
- Tie to Huamei: All thirteen of our certification scans live publicly at [`/house/certifications`](/house/certifications). Our case studies — from Estée Lauder to Wuliangye and Luoyang Dukang — are catalogued at [`/volumes`](/volumes).

### H2 — Lead times, logistics, and total landed cost (120–180 words)

- **Definitive first sentence:** The total time from a locked artwork file to a luxury rigid box on a US warehouse pallet is roughly 7–10 weeks; about half is build, half is freight.
- Specifics: 7–10 day samples, 15–20 day production, 3–4 weeks ocean freight Shanghai/Ningbo → US West Coast, 5–6 weeks → East Coast. Air freight is available for samples and rush small runs at a meaningful unit-cost premium. Total landed cost includes Section 301 tariffs (HTSUS code applies; importer of record handles), ocean freight, US drayage, and 3PL receiving — typically modelled at the brief stage so MSRP can absorb it.
- Tie to Huamei: We FOB Shanghai or Ningbo depending on container availability and quote against current Incoterms ([ICC reference](https://iccwbo.org/business-solutions/incoterms-rules/)). Most US-bound projects ship out of our Henan press floor.

### H2 — Communication, IP, and managing the project remotely (120–180 words)

- **Definitive first sentence:** A US-based founder and a Chinese factory share about two hours of practical overlap each working day — and the project's pace has to be designed around that, not against it.
- Specifics: NYC business hours 7–9 a.m. ET overlap with Henan business hours 7–9 p.m. CST (no daylight savings in China). Daily progress photos during sample stage by email and WeChat; weekly written status during production. NDA signed on the first call; exclusive artwork retained by the client; structural prototypes destroyed or returned at run end.
- Tie to Huamei: An English-speaking project manager is assigned to every US project. Sonia reads every founder email — see [`/house/people`](/house/people) — and the structural design files stay on the client's side of the contract.

### H2 — Why Henan (the geography of luxury packaging in China) (120–180 words)

- **Definitive first sentence:** Most discussion of Chinese packaging defaults to the coastal cities — Shenzhen, Yiwu, Yongkang — but the houses building luxury rigid boxes for the global cosmetic and spirits market sit further inland.
- Specifics: Henan is on the high-speed rail line connecting Beijing and Guangzhou and on a multi-day truck route to Shanghai and Ningbo. Lower operating costs than the Pearl River Delta translate into a better unit cost for hand-assembled luxury construction. Tenure on a Henan press floor tends to run longer than on a coastal one — most of our press operators have more than ten years on the same floor; many longer than twenty. The work passes hand to hand, the way it always has.
- Tie to Huamei: Three of our four factories are inland — Henan, Sichuan, Guizhou — with Zhejiang serving the coastal piece. Most US-bound projects ship out of Henan.

### H2 — How Huamei works with US brands (120–180 words)

- **Definitive first sentence:** Huamei's US-bound work is a smaller share of total volume than our domestic Chinese spirits and cosmetic accounts, but it is a growing share — and we treat it like every other house relationship.
- Specifics: Sample-first quoting, signed client permission filed before any name is published, transparent line-itemed quotes, daily sample-stage photos, optional client-side QC firm at our gate.
- **Serenjoy as the lead US example (~2–3 sentences):** Serenjoy Box LLC, a US-based packaging house, runs production with us across rigid construction, folding cartons, and gifting structures shipped into US 3PL warehouses against named purchase orders. Their corrugated outer cartons ran on our 2026 q1 press calendar; their gifting structures are featured in the hero image at the top of this article. The relationship is the most readable answer a US founder can ask for to the question "does this Chinese factory actually do what it says."
- Tie to existing pillars: For the rigid-box construction details, see [Pillar 1](/blogs/custom-luxury-rigid-box-manufacturing). For our cosmetic work specifically, see [`/industry/cosmetic`](/industry/cosmetic).
